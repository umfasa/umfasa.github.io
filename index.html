FASA

University of Michigan

Filipino American Student Association

A diverse group of individuals united by the Filipino culture's love for new experiences, peoples, and foods, this family of individuals from all over the world partake in the adventures of college together, supporting each other through the rough times and experiencing the great times together.

There is a plethora of personalities and characters that you'll discover and connect with on a deep, personal level. In this family, we value every individual's input and participation, each bringing his or her own flavor to the optimistic and energetic environment we aspire to produce.

With a wide variety of activities, we're sure you'll be able to find your niche in FASA

We hope you can join the family
