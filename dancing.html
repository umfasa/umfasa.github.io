Dance

No experience necessary

FASA Dance is a group where its members bond over a shared love of traditional and modern dance. Through traditional Filipino dances, FASA Traditional strives to demonstrate and inform others of the different subcultures that exist in the Philippines. FASA Modern includes experienced dancers, but many are completely new to it. Because many of today's most popular hip-hop dance crews and several musical artists are Filipino, modern dance is an integral part of today's Filipino culture, and FASA wishes to celebrate the great diversity that the culture represents.
