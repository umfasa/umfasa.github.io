Sports

Defend the Rice Bowl

What better way to get to know the people of FASA then through some friendly competition? Meet us at the CCRB for weekly basketball games, the occasional volleyball tournament, or any other kind of physical activity that interests you. FASA was the defending champion (three years running) of the Rice Bowl, an annual sports competition held with MSU PASS. We play nicely, we promise.
