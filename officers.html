Executive Board

As representatives of FASA's interests, executive board members collate the ideas of FASA members, external organizations, and the University of Michigan into one place. Responsible for the planning and success of events, every board member is always willing to sit down to hear and discuss anything anyone has to say, whether it be a concern or a friendly discussion. Send your suggestions to board!
