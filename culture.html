Culture

Experience the Philippines

What brings FASA together? Our shared culture and heritage, of course! Our national hero, Jose Rizal, once said “Ang hindi marunong lumingon sa pinaggalingan ay hindi makararating sa paroroonan. (He who does not look back where he came from will not reach his destination).” In other words, your culture is part of who you are, and knowing your own culture and heritage will help you succeed in life! FASA’s cultural activities aim to do just this, by bringing its members together to share the culture and traditions that brought us together in the first place!

Try the delicious, tasty foods that the Philippines has.

Participate in some of the Philippine's traditional dances, such as dance colloquially known as "Coconut Boys".

Satiate your sweet tooth with some of the best savory desserts of the Philipines, such as leche flan.
